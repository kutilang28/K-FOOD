About Us

When we entered the Indonesian market, we were conducting market research for the purpose of selling food machinery. We saw that food was being manufactured and sold in unhygienic and poor environments. If we launched and manufactured and sold food products with Korean products, we would have sufficient growth in Indonesia's population and economic growth. judged to be marketable.
